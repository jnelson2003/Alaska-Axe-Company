## 👤 User Experience (UX) Blueprint

Design decisions are heavily driven by the principles found in **Laws of UX** (e.g., Hick's Law, Serial Position Effect, Jakob's Law).

### 🎯 Core User Intent
Most visitors are trying to do exactly one of three things:
1. **Book an experience**
2. **Plan a group event**
3. **Figure out what this place is**

### 🔄 The Continuous Booking Funnel
The site will continually guide users toward the primary conversion goal: **Booking**. Every informational section ends with a relevant contextual CTA.
> *   Finished reading about parties? ➡️ **[Book Event]**
> *   Viewing the gallery? ➡️ **[Reserve a Lane]**
> *   Reading the FAQ? ➡️ **[Ready to Throw?]**

### 📦 Consolidated Content Strategy
Instead of overwhelming users with six nearly identical pages for every party type, **Groups & Events** is housed on a single, highly scannable page using distinct sections for:
*   Birthday Parties • Corporate Events • Team Building • Date Nights • Bachelor/Bachelorette • Wedding Parties

## 📈 SEO & Local Discovery Strategy

To maximize visibility in the Fairbanks market, the site targets broad "intent-based" local keywords rather than just "axe throwing."

### 🔑 Targeted Blog Content ("Things To Do" Hub)
We will publish optimized content targeting high-volume local searches:
*   *Things to Do in Fairbanks*
*   *Indoor Activities in Fairbanks*
*   *Fun Date Ideas in Fairbanks*
*   *Rainy Day Activities in Fairbanks*
*   *Team Building Activities in Fairbanks*

### 🏷️ Title Tag Schema
Every page title must follow a strict, keyword-first optimization structure:
*   **Homepage:** `Axe Throwing in Fairbanks, Alaska | Alaska Axe Throwing Co.`
*   **Events Page:** `Corporate Team Building in Fairbanks | Alaska Axe Throwing Co.`

### 🖼️ Asset Optimization
*   **Favicon/Logo:** High-res mini logo optimized for Google Search Snippets.
*   **Alt Text:** All images must use descriptive, keyword-conscious alt text (e.g., `<img src="lane-1.jpg" alt="Friends enjoying interactive axe throwing lanes at Alaska Axe Throwing Co in Fairbanks">`).
